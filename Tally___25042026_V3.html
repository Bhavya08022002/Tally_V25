The UAE Federal Tax Authority is rolling out Peppol-based e-invoicing in phases: pilot from 1 July 2026, mandatory for AED 50 Mn+ businesses from 1 January 2027, full SME rollout by July 2027. Zoho Books is the cloud-native incumbent well positioned for the mandate. Wafeq is purpose-built for Arabic UX and GCC compliance. Both can reach Tally's own UAE customers with a migration offer first — the single biggest execution risk.

② Ship Corporate Tax computation — UAE Corporate Tax (9%) has been live since June 2023; close this gap.

③ One-click desktop-to-cloud — remove the biggest migration objection with a single-click tool.
